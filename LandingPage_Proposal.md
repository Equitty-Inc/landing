# Executive Summary

This document is the official website communication strategy for EQUITTY during the pre-launch phase -- the period between now and the granting of our CNAD Digital Asset Service Provider (DASP) license.

The current equitty.com landing page, while well-intentioned, triggered a cease-and-desist from the Comision Nacional de Activos Digitales (CNAD) because it was perceived as soliciting investors before the platform is licensed to do so. This proposal replaces that approach entirely.

The goal of this redesign is threefold:

- Stay fully compliant with CNAD requirements -- no solicitation, no premature offers
- Build credibility, trust, and awareness with potential investors and asset partners
- Generate documented proof of market validation that supports our seed fundraise

**COMPLIANCE NOTE: Critical Compliance Requirement**

No page, section, button, or form on equitty.com may imply that investments are currently available, that users are being enrolled as investors, or that EQUITTY holds any license to operate a digital asset marketplace. Every piece of copy must be reviewed through this lens before going live.

The strategy that follows replaces the waitlist with a compliance-safe 'Company Preview' model: EQUITTY presents itself as a company under construction -- transparent about what it is building, who is building it, why the timing is right, and where it stands regulatorily. Interest from investors and partners is captured through a newsletter subscription (not a waitlist), clearly framed as an informational update -- not an offer or solicitation.

### Our Compliance Architecture

**1\. Regulatory First-Mover**

EQUITTY is applying for the DASP license under El Salvador's Ley de Activos Digitales -- the only end-to-end digital asset law in Latin America. We are not operating on a legal gray area. We are operating on the law.

**2\. Dual-Jurisdiction Structure**

EQUITTY Holdings Inc. (Delaware C-Corp) owns EQUITTY S.A.S. de C.V. (El Salvador). This dual structure provides institutional-grade governance through U.S. law while operating under El Salvador's favorable digital asset framework.

**3\. Investor Protection by Design**

Assets will be structured through dedicated Delaware SPVs. Capital deployment will follow milestone-based release triggers. KYC/AML will be embedded before any funds move. Each offering will be filed with CNAD under the applicable private tokenization framework.

**4\. Transparency as Policy**

EQUITTY is committed to disclosing its regulatory status clearly and publicly at all times -- including on this page. When the DASP license is granted, we will announce it. When phase milestones are reached, we will share them. We do not operate in the dark.

### Who Can Invest When We Launch

**Important: Investor Access at Launch**

When EQUITTY launches its first offering (targeted Q3 2026, subject to DASP approval), access will initially be restricted to authorized and qualified investors as defined by applicable law under the CNAD framework. General public retail access will follow in a subsequent phase. We are not accepting investor applications, expressions of interest, or capital commitments at this time.
